The entire team at INSIGNIA is happy to be a beneficiary of the Filecoin/IPFS Next Step Microgrant. Designed to support taking the next step after building an initial prototype with Filecoin, Insignia uses the Interplanetary File System to store all game assets decentrally.

The project was a Hackathon participant sponsored by Filecoin/IPFS and hosted by Encode Club in late 2021. Initially known as Mage Wars, the project won the first prize in the "Bridging Challenge" category and the Runner-up Prize in the "Unleash Challenge" category. This partly qualified the project for the Encode/Filecoin Accelerator Program.

It was a 10-week, online accelerator taking the best hackathon and early-stage projects worldwide and helping them take the leap to become fully-fledged startups. Hosted by Encode Club and sponsored by Filecoin.

About Filecoin

Filecoin, the world’s largest decentralized network, allow users to store, request, and transfer data via a verifiable marketplace. Filecoin is completely open-source, enabling people from all over the world to participate.

The Filecoin protocol runs on an advanced and novel proof system — a combination of Proof of Spacetime (PoSt) and Proof of Replication (PoRep), to provide unmatched efficiency and reliability. Filecoin’s advanced technology provides a robust foundation to store the world’s most valuable data sets.

An alternative to costly cloud storage, the Filecoin network offers efficiently priced and geographically decentralized storage, minimizing financial barriers and allowing users to take advantage of its unmatched network capabilities.

About Encode Club

Encode is a leading web3 education community. Its mission is to help ambitious, talented people achieve their personal and professional web3 goals. Encode does this through organizing high-quality programs including hackathons, coding boot camps, educational workshops, and accelerators in partnership with the leading blockchain protocols. Once participating in their program, they help people get hired through their dedicated recruitment arm or receive investment through their investment fund.

Contrary to the "Appear and Disappear" syndrome of many projects in the space, Insignia is growing organically by leveraging the expertise and experience of seasoned Advisors. One of the problems plaguing the web3 and crypto space is one we have all unfortunately become too familiar with. Projects suddenly appear from the blues, create an insane amount of hype, and pump their token, just to disappear after a brief period.

We are creating a verifiable track record towards building a sustainable product and creating value for the community.
