About SORCERERS

SORCERERS is an immersive, AAA, 3D military strategy blockchain game that pays to play. Born out of the need to solve the known problems currently plaguing the blockchain gaming ecosystem, SORCERERS is at the melting point of NFT, DEFI and 3D gaming. A multi-platform and multi-protocol play-and-earn blockchain application with enjoyable game-play built with Unreal Engine. Players have the opportunity to own unique digital assets, not only valuable but have real in-game use case. SORCERERS is also implementing the concept of "collateral-less" NFT Leasing for non-stop, full-circle earning potential. Players can now earn while they play their favourite game and when they are not playing. SORCERERS is not sacrificing fun at the expense of reward. The game hopes to achieve a fine balance between fun and reward.
